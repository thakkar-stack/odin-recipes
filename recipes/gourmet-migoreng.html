Gourmet Migoreng

Description:

Gourmet Migoreng made with spicy red chillis, seasoned tofu and nutrition filled mushrooms!

Time: 15 minutes Makes: 2 servings Calories: ~350

Ingredients:

  • 2 packets instant noodles with seasoning
  • 2 Fresh red or green chillis
  • 200 grams firm tofu
  • 1/4 tsp of each powder:

    - Chilli, salt, pepper and garam masala

  • 1 Chopped full tomato
  • 1 Diced red onion
  • 10 chopped mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp oil

Method:

  1. Take a pot and boil 400ml of water. Once water is at a rolling boil, add noodles to water and cook until soft. Set aside for later.
  2. Take a pan, add oil and heat over medium flame. Add tofu to oil and stir fry until softened. Add seasoning to tofu, and set aside.
  3. Add more oil if needed and bring up to temperature, throw in onion, chillis & mushrooms. Saute and add noodle seasoning. Mix in tomatoes and let simmer.
  4. After 2 minutes add tofu into the mix and bring up to heat.
  5. Finally, add in the noodles and mix well.
  6. Serve while hot.
